Technical Info

For this photo I used a 10 stop ND filter from NiSi to extend my shutter speed to 1 minute and 34 seconds. To get everything tack sharp front to back I focused on three diferent points. As the wind was picking a bit I did a high ISO exposure to freeze the flowers. Finally I did a quick exposure of 1/2s to get motion in the waterfall.
